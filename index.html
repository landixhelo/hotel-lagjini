HOTEL LAGJINI

It's not Perfect... It's Paradise

Providing mountain views, Hotel Lagjini in Lukovë offers accommodations, a garden, and a terrace. Butrint National Park is within 23 miles of the condo hotel. The condo hotel provides rooms with air conditioning, free private parking, and free Wifi.

Overlooking the sea, rooms in this condo hotel are accessible via private entrance and fitted with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hot tub and slippers. At the condo hotel, all units are soundproof. The rooms are equipped with heating facilities.
